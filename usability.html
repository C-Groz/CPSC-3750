Usability Test Results - Audio Changer App

Link to app before changes: Audio Changer V1

Link to app after changes: Audio Changer V2

Test Summary

Tester: My roommate, Will Rosia who is a tech-savvy Clemson student with little programming experience who loves music

Location: Our kitchen area, on my laptop (Chrome browser)

Date: April 26, 2025

Test Tasks and User Performance

Task User Performance Notes
Play and pause the song Completed the task in 5 seconds Will stated that this task was very easy because he is used to completing this task in other music software
Create a title named "Task" after the 2-minute mark in the song Completed the task in 30 seconds Will took a moment to figure out how to navigate through the song. Had minor difficulty figuring out how to add a title
Delete the "Task" title Will completed this task in 15 seconds Will took time for will to figure out that he has to click on the title first to delete it

Pain Points and Confusions

  • - Confused what the word title meant
  • - Difficulty figuring out how to delete title
  • - Difficult to read button names due to small font/coloring

Actionable Improvements

  • - Created detailed instructions for how to use the app and changed the word title to timestamp
  • - Created a delete button on each timestamp for easy deletion
  • - Increased font size and changed color scheme
